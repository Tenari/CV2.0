Name alert:
  indicates to the server the name of the character being controlled by this client.
  "N [name]"

Move North:
  indicates that the player wants to move 'up' or 'north' one square.
  "W"

Move South:
  indicates that the player wants to move 'down' or 'south' one square.
  "S"

Move East:
  indicates that the player wants to move 'right' or 'east' one square.
  "D"

Move West:
  indicates that the player wants to move 'left' or 'west' one square.
  "A"
